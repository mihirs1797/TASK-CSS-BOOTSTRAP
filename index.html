Location
The sudden drop in the price of milk has resulted in a country-wide protest from the committee of cows. The committe claims that the cows weren't insured and the sudden removal from their jobs has put them in state of udder shock.
A member of the cow committe had this to say - "Oh moo god, I do not know what to say. I did not ever think I would have to pick a beef with this company. I am going to take legal action and I know the steaks are high but I just cannot let it go as spilt milk!"
